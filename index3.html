Product description

SMS Pro integration for Creatio is a connector for send and receive individual messages and also send mass mailings.

Use cases:

The connector is suitable for your company if you need to send a mass mailing to a group of contacts or if you want to receive a reply to a message. You will be able to manage message statuses, receive graphic statistics on sent and received messages.


Also set a timer for sending SMS. With the help of the "Create SMS with cards" subprocess, you can display your own business process on any section in the Creatio system and send an SMS at a certain point in production.

Key features:

  • Sending mass messages to a group of contacts
  • You will receive a status for each contact, whether the message was delivered
  • You will receive a status for each contact, whether the message was delivered
  • Configure the business process to send an SMS after any activity in the Creatio system
  • You can set the necessary time for sending SMS
  • You can receive and send messages from unknown numbers and create new potential customers in the process
  • The presence of the functionality of trigger messages. With the help of macros, you can substitute the name of the client, or the amount to be paid, or the account number, etc

To use the connector, you must be registered in the TWILIO account
